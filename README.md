# LinkUp — Access Guide

LinkUp is a social-first group event planning app built as a GW capstone project. It helps friends plan hangouts together with shared events, polls, tasks, itineraries, and group chat.

---

## 🌐 How to Access

LinkUp is a **web application** that works on any modern browser — desktop, tablet, or mobile. No installation required.

### Live App
👉 **https://linkup4210.lovable.app**

### Supported Devices
- **Desktop / Laptop**: Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge (latest versions)
- **Mobile**: Open the link in your phone's browser (Safari on iOS, Chrome on Android). The interface is fully responsive.
- **Tablet**: Works the same as desktop in your browser.

> 💡 **Tip for mobile users**: For an app-like experience, tap your browser's "Share" menu and choose **"Add to Home Screen"**. LinkUp will then launch full-screen like a native app.

## 🧭 What to Try First

Once logged in, here are the main areas to explore:

| Page | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Events** | Browse upcoming and past events. Click any event to see details, RSVP, chat, tasks, polls, and itinerary. |
| **Calendar** | See all your scheduled events and your task list grouped by event. |
| **Friends** | Search for friends, accept requests, or invite people via email/SMS. |
| **Messages** | Direct and group chats with friends. |
| **Profile** | View your activity wrapped, manage your account, or upgrade your plan. |

## 📝 Creating Your Own Account

Prefer to start fresh? Click **Sign Up** and provide:
- A unique **username** (3–30 characters)
- A valid **email address**
- A **password** with at least 8 characters, including a letter, a number, and a special character

You can also sign up instantly with **Google**.

---

## 💬 Free vs. Premium

LinkUp is free to use with these limits:
- 1 event per month
- Up to 5 groups
- Up to 6 people per group
- Live task updates require Premium

Upgrade anytime from the **Upgrade** page in your profile menu.

## ❓ Troubleshooting

- **"Wrong username or password"** — double-check capitalization. The demo password is `Demo123!` (capital D, exclamation mark at the end).
- **Page won't load** — refresh the browser, or try an incognito/private window.
- **Mobile layout looks off** — make sure you're using the latest version of Safari or Chrome.
